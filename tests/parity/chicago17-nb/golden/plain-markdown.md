# Fixture: chicago17-nb parity

The argument developed by Jane A. Smith[^1] ran counter to earlier claims about
pragmatic markers in contact situations. Smith's later treatment[^2]
extended the point with closer reading of the data. The parallel case
addressed in Robert Jones and Elena Cruz[^3] reinforced it;[^4]
supplied the numerical support. Recent work in the area[^5]
broadened the empirical base.

A consolidated framing appears in.[^6]
