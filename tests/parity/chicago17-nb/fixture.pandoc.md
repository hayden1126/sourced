# Fixture: chicago17-nb parity

The argument developed by @smith-2010 ran counter to earlier claims about
pragmatic markers in contact situations. Smith's later treatment [@smith-2010]
extended the point with closer reading of the data. The parallel case
addressed in @jones-cruz-2015 reinforced it; [@jones-cruz-2015, p. 88]
supplied the numerical support. Recent work in the area [@able-baker-chen-2019]
broadened the empirical base.

A consolidated framing appears in [@smith-2010; @jones-cruz-2015; @able-baker-chen-2019].
